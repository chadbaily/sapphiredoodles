Family Dogs

Golden-Doodles are known for being great family dogs. They combine the happy and lovey attitude of a golden retriever with the intelligence and hypoallergenic features of a poodle.

Friendly

Each puppy gorws up with lots of human and animal interaciton. They will be a great addition to any family, or a friend to another family pet!

Hypoallergenic

Because the golden doodle is a mix between a golden retriever and a poodle, they are hypoallergenic. This means that they will shed very little or not at all. This allows the dogs to be in households where people with mild allergies are present and not be affected.
